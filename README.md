# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee data to uncover workforce trends, attrition patterns, salary distribution, and employee satisfaction using Excel and Power BI.

The goal is to help HR teams identify major factors influencing employee turnover and improve workforce planning.

---

## Tools Used

* Excel (Data Cleaning & EDA)
* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains employee-level HR information including:

* Age
* Gender
* Department
* Job Role
* Monthly Income
* Attrition
* Business Travel
* Overtime
* Job Satisfaction
* Work-Life Balance

---

## Key Metrics

* Total Employees: 1470
* Employees Left: 237
* Attrition Rate: 16.12%
* Average Salary: 6.50K
* Average Age: 36.92

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 — HR Overview

Includes:

* KPI Cards

* Attrition by Age Group
* Average Salary by Job Role

### Page 2 — Employee Insights

Includes:

* Job Role Distribution
* Salary Distribution
* Overtime Impact on Attrition
* Business Travel Impact
* Job Satisfaction Analysis
* Work-Life Balance Analysis

---

## Key Insights

* Research & Development has the highest attrition.
* Male employees show higher attrition than female employees.
* Mid-career employees leave more frequently.
* Employees with overtime show higher attrition.
* Frequent business travel increases attrition risk.
* Better job satisfaction correlates with employee retention.
